About The Project

Do you have a dream? How will you make it come true?

My dream will require some hard work. I know I MUST be:

  • Organized
  • Well prepared
  • Plan ahead

We started this project as a course project. Step by step it became our dream, this calendar is built by programmers. One of its many traits is the ability to add features.

  • Want to monitor your expenses? We got it.
  • Want to organize a zoom meeting while still under quarantine? We got it.

Even better - you got it! Develop your own feature and add it to the calendar. This calendar is open source developed mostly in Python as the finale to an extraordinary course. For the calendar development we selected features, aiming at required and unique. Then each of us began the journey to implement it in the calendar. We worked collaboratively to put it together.
